Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic treat that’s easy to make and loved by everyone!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour and baking soda.
  3. In another bowl, cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
  4. Add eggs to the butter mixture and mix well.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture and stir until combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
